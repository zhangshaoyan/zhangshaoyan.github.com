VIVRE LE FILM

Film projectionist Old Li is retiring in a few days, and the movie theatre which he serves will go digital soon.

However, Old Li finds an indifferent attitude towards film and projection work on Qi, a shiftless young man who is taking over the job only because it’s relaxing.

In order to evoke Qi’s passion, Old Li decides to tell the story of his most special screening in life. In the screening, Old Li operated his projector for an audience of one, a ten-year-old boy, while a coalmine collapses around them.

After hearing Old Li’s story, Qi starts to realize the importance of film and how huge its impact on a projectionist can be, and decides to treat projection work with all his heart.
