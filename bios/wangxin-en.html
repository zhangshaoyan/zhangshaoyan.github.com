WANG Xin - Art Director

Wang Xin is a graduate of Hubei University holding a Bachelor’s Degree of Arts majoring in English language study. After graduation, Wang Xin joined DongFeng Automobile Co., Ltd, working as journalist and a Chinese-English translator. Two years later, Wang Xin arrived in Yunnan and started working as an editor for "Che" Fashion Magazine. Later, she joined Kunming Daily.

In 2011, Wang Xin joined New York University’s master program of Animation and Digital Arts.

Wang Xin formed her interest for art in her early childhood through watching Disney Cartoons. She has twelve years painting experience. So far, she has worked on more than 10 animation projects of different types such as stop motion, flash, 3D modeling, interactive animation, game design, etc.

In early 2012, Wang Xin met her mentor Hans Bacher (production consultant of "Beauty and the Beast", character designer of "The Lion King", production designer of "Mulan"). Wang Xin is currently developing her own projects "The Alien’s First Step" and "Sketch Book" under the supervision of Hans.
