WANG Xin - Art Director

Wang Xin is a graduate of Hubei University holding a Bachelor’s Degree of Arts specializing in English language study. After graduation, Wang Xin joined DongFeng Automobile Co., Ltd working as a journalist as well as Chinese-English translator. Two years later, she arrived in Yunnan and started her career as an editor in "Che" Fashion Magazine. Later, she joined Kunming Daily.

In 2011, Wang Xin joined New York University’s master program of Animation and Digital Arts.

Wang Xin’s interest for art started in her early childhood by watching Disney Cartoons. She has twelve years painting experience. So far, she has worked on more than 10 animation projects of different types including stop motion, flash, 3D modeling, interactive animation and game design.

Early this year, Wang Xin met her mentor Hans Bacher (production consultant of “Beauty and the Beast”, character designer of “The Lion King”, production designer of “Mulan”). Wang Xin is currently developing her own projects “The Alien’s First Step” and “Sketch Book” under the supervision of Hans.
